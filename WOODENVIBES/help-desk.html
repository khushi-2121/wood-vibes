How can I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing through our products, adding items to your cart, and completing the checkout process. You'll need to provide your delivery address and payment details. Orders are confirmed via email once payment is successful.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused and in original packaging. Please initiate a return request from your "My Orders" page with a photo of the product and packaging. Once approved, we'll arrange pickup and process your refund within 5-7 business days.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location. Generally, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days from the date of order placement. You can track your order status in real-time using the "Track Order" feature. Expedited delivery options may be available in select areas.

Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes! All our wooden kitchenware is made from sustainably sourced wood. We use non-toxic finishes and environmentally friendly packaging. Each product is handcrafted with care to ensure durability and longevity, reducing the need for replacements.

How do I care for my wooden products?

Hand wash your wooden items with mild soap and warm water. Dry immediately with a soft cloth. Apply food-grade mineral oil occasionally to keep the wood nourished and prevent cracking. Avoid soaking or using a dishwasher. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Do you offer bulk orders or corporate gifts?

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and corporate gifting. Please contact our sales team at sales@woodenvibes.com with your requirements. We can provide custom packaging and personalization options for bulk orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, UPI transfers, net banking, and digital wallets. We also offer Cash on Delivery (COD) in select areas. All payments are secure and encrypted.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order anytime by visiting our "Track Order" page and entering your Order ID or the phone number associated with your account. You'll see real-time updates of your order status from placement to delivery.
